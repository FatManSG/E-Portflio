Arduino Air Quality Monitoring

Jul 2024

Me and my team developed a lab-environment monitoring system using Arduino microcontrollers, MQ/temperature/humidity sensors, and MQTT streaming into a MySQL backend. In Python (Jupyter) I parsed CSV exports, cleaned missing/duplicate readings, and engineered time-series features before publishing to a Power BI dashboard. The dashboard revealed weekend spikes in temperature and humidity—prompting a recommendation for weekend-only cooling interventions to protect equipment and personnel.

Business Data Analysis Presentation

Jul 2024

I led the “Singapore Tourism Analysis” engagement, sourcing 2008–2023 arrival, spending, and demographic data from SingStat’s TableBuilder. Using Python (pandas/NumPy) I cleaned, normalized, and reshaped multiple tables, then visualized key insights in Power BI. We identified the 25–54 Y.O. age bracket (especially visitors from China, India, Malaysia, Indonesia), peak arrival months (Q3–Q4), and top spending category (hotels, 49% of accommodation) . A low correlation (0.18) between arrivals and receipts signaled a “revenge tourism” effect—with higher per-capita spend post-pandemic—informing targeted promotions and dynamic pricing strategies to boost receipts further.

Leaf Sense - Plant Disease Detection

Oct 2024

Me and 2 other teammates grouped together to build LeafSense, a responsive web app (HTML/CSS/JavaScript) that empowers farmers to diagnose crop diseases via computer vision. Uploaded leaf images are preprocessed with OpenCV (background masking) before being classified by a DenseNet121 model trained on 20 000+ samples. Results are returned in under 2 s, alongside an educational module detailing each disease and treatment. This solution targets early intervention, helping to mitigate yield losses and bolster food-security efforts in rural communities.

OptiFlow - IoT Crowd Control

Sep 2024

I designed and implemented OptiFlow with a team of 3, an end-to-end crowd-management prototype that uses a Raspberry Pi, ultrasonic distance sensors, an infrared Pi Camera running a YOLOv8 model, and servo-driven gates to dynamically regulate queues. Live foot-traffic counts from the camera and distance readings from the sensors feed into both an on-device LCD display and a Power BI dashboard for remote monitoring. The system autonomously opens/closes barriers based on density thresholds, demonstrating scalable, real-time congestion control and improving user flow and safety in high-traffic environments.

FESI-Crew - Repeat Buyer Classification

Apr 2025

I contributed to building a Dockerized ML pipeline that ingests and cleans raw Olist data, trains a Random Forest model, and packages everything for easy deployment. First, data engineers scripts in PySpark handled missing values, merged orders/reviews/delivery logs, and generated RFM and timing features. Next, a training module ran automated hyperparameter tuning with Optuna to optimize model performance, achieving a 0.92 F1 score on unseen data. Finally, the entire workflow—including the cleaning scripts, training code, and model artifact—was containerized in Docker, enabling one-click startup of the end-to-end pipeline.

High-End PC Build – Performance Workstation

Jun 2025

I did a end-to-end assembly and tuning of a custom workstation engineered for machine-learning model training and everyday productivity. The system is built around an AMD Ryzen 9 9950X3D CPU paired with an NVIDIA RTX 5090 GPU, 96 GB of DDR5-6400 RAM running at full XMP profiles, on an ASUS ROG Strix X870E-E Gaming WiFi (AM5) motherboard, all powered by a Corsair HXi 1500 W PSU housed in a Lian Li O11 Airflow 217 chassis. I optimized cable routing and case airflow for efficient cooling, resolved an initial Windows boot failure via a clean USB reinstall, and enabled maximum memory bandwidth in the BIOS. Finally, I validated rock-solid reliability under sustained full-load conditions using OCCT stress tests. The result is a stable, high-throughput platform that accelerates intensive training jobs and delivers seamless day-to-day performance.
