Business Data Analysis

Led a data analysis on Singapore tourism using Python and Power BI. Revealed age and season-based spending trends and suggested pricing strategies based on “revenge tourism” behavior.

Long Description

I led the “Singapore Tourism Analysis” engagement, sourcing 2008–2023 arrival, spending, and demographic data from SingStat’s TableBuilder. Using Python (pandas/NumPy) I cleaned, normalized, and reshaped multiple tables, then visualized key insights in Power BI. We identified the 25–54 Y.O. age bracket (especially visitors from China, India, Malaysia, Indonesia), peak arrival months (Q3–Q4), and top spending category (hotels, 49% of accommodation) . A low correlation (0.18) between arrivals and receipts signaled a “revenge tourism” effect—with higher per-capita spend post-pandemic—informing targeted promotions and dynamic pricing strategies to boost receipts further.

Technical Highlights

  • Cleaned and joined 2008–2023 data from SingStat TableBuilder
  • Used pandas/NumPy for reshaping and analysis
  • Power BI dashboards to reveal age and seasonal trends
